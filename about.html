Centre for Sustainable Fashion — Who we are

Launched in 2008 in response to an over–whelming sense that much of contemporary fashion reflected a society locked in patterns of excessive consumption and cultural disconnect.

Our starting point was sustainability as a lens to view the world of fashion, challenging both artistic and business practice. Our goal was and still is to create better lives for those involved in and affected by our work, whilst ensuring resilience by living within ecological limits.

We have grown from a tiny team to a diverse community of world leading researchers, designers, educators and communicators with an extensive network that crosses disciplines, generations, cultures and locations.
